Common Questions

1) How to post a job?
Click on Post Job button and fill work type, budget, address and details.
2) How to assign a worker?
Go to Accepted Workers section → click Assign.
3) How to change language/theme?
Open sidebar menu → click Settings icon → choose Language or Theme.
